Common Mistakes

Bad: Image without alt text

Screen readers can't describe this image to blind users:

<img src="photo.jpg"> - Missing alt attribute!

Good: Image with descriptive alt text

<img src="photo.jpg" alt="Students working on laptops in computer lab">
